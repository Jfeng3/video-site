NEWS

 

 

 
 
 

PEEQ is creating a new market opportunity for capturing video highlights in amateur sports. Our system intelligently captures and automatically edits video into ready to watch highlights. Content is distributed over the cloud directly to any viewing destination, including TV, mobile or social networks.

 

 
 
 

 

How it works

Using our patent pending system, teams and facilities can capture, edit, distribute and monetize video highlights.

Mobile phones are placed around the field.

iPhones mounted to tripods are placed where the action is on the field, allowing PEEQ to get the best possible angle.

Players wear PEEQ trackers during the game.

With a selection of attachments designed for any situation, players can keep their eye on the ball with zero distraction.

Trackers tell our mobile app when players are in view.

With a Bluetooth range of 200 feet and up to 2 days of battery life, the PEEQ tracker is designed to handle even the toughest playing field.
